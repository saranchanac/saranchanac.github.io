Personal Project

Senior Project : Development of Trajectory and Motion Controllers for AGV

King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thenburi • Gensurv.co
JUNE 2016 - DEC 2016

This project is the software development of trajectory control and motion control for Automatic Guided Vehicle (AGV). This AGV use in the factory for reduce the problem of limited human labor, reduce the mistakes and accidents that can happen to humans. The trajectory control associate position data from the laser navigation device with target waypoints, the trajectory controller compute the appropriate velocity of each wheel which drive the AGV along the desired waypoints. The velocities of the left and right wheels are determined by the kinematics model of two-wheeled differential drive with PID position control algorithm. Then these velocities are feed to the motion controller via serial communication port. The motion controller controls the speed of the motor connected to the wheel to achieve the requested velocity. This speed control is implemented by PID speed control algorithm. The profermance of this AGV can move smooth with maximum load 80 kg. with speed more than 0.5 m/s and deviation from line is lower than 0.6 m.
